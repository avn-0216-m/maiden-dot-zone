Jealousy

You are an imitation of a popular brand, not made to last, but you were so much better than the originals. The others are too small and floppy. You are the perfect size to nestle against me every night while I sleep. I spend long restless nights looking for any trace of you online, but your lineage is scarce and in disarray, and your label is faded, impossible to read now. All the websites I scour tell me you're out of stock, save for the seediest no-name scam storefronts in the deepest depths of my search results. I don't want to replace you, not ever, but if I ever lost you... That would be the moment my heart rips in two. I know you're falling apart, but can't you love me a little longer, like you have for so long?
